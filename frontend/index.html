ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Brett! I'm a dedicated engineer with a passion for Azure and Microsoft 365 services. Currently, I’m a frontline engineer for Teams Rooms and Devices with a Microsoft partner, where I provide critical support and insights to help improve Microsoft's products and services.

My IT journey began in 2011 during college, leading me to work at a small consulting firm that was later acquired by an MSP. After four years there, I spent nearly three more at another consulting firm before the pandemic reshaped the landscape (a total of 9 years of experience).

In 2021, I dove into the world of cloud-based systems, determined to bridge knowledge gaps and stay ahead in today’s rapidly evolving tech climate. With almost a decade of experience in the IT industry, I specialize in a broad spectrum of Microsoft platforms. However, my curiosity and enthusiasm extend to Linux-based systems as well, where I explore open-source solutions that prioritize privacy and security.

Outside of work, you’ll find me playing guitar, mountain biking, at the gym, or tinkering in my homelab—where I self-host apps and ensure my irreplaceable documents and photos are securely backed up.

Life is a journey, and with a relentless drive for growth and knowledge, I believe we can all continuously explore and learn together.
